| Our Mission



Our mission is to solve the inequality in the retail market to built a even platform for all market participants




Retail


Giving local shops the opportunity to list their products online is at the heart of our mission. Level the playing field for online sale is what we strive for.


Local


People want local products and miss the touch and feel of the products. Econdo connects both parties on a platform to strengthen local economies and make the shopping experience better.

| FAQ




We are starting with basic household appliances and stationery products. Over time we aim to include all kinds of products from your neighborhood to broaden your search. Let us know which products you would like to see.



Write an email to info@econdo.de with the details and we will work the whole return process. Once your product has arrived back at the shop, the team will inspect it and you will get your refund.



Guarantee depends on the single shops policies. When buying we will let you know if there is a guarantee period and in case how long it is.
